FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS



DELIVERY SELF COLLECT ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

Delivery

1) How do I order GFC Delivery online?

Just follow these simple steps:

Click on Order Now ----->Select Delivery ----->Enter your delivery address ----->Select items from the Delivery Menu----->Checkout, pay and we'll deliver!

2) Can I make changes or cancel my order after placing an order?

You may change or cancel your order via our delivery, provided that the ordered items have not left the restaurant.

3) How will I know if GFC has received my online order?

An order confirmation email will be sent to your registered email address once your order has been submitted.

4) What do I do if my order arrived with missing items?

Please call our delivery hotline at 1234-252-888 to inform our customer service personnels of your missing items. We will arrange for the items to be delivered to you in the soonest time possible.

5) When is the operating hours for GFC Delivery?

GFC Delivery is available from 8.00am to 10.00pm from Mondays to Sundays including public holidays. However, delivery hours may vary depending on stores.


Self Collect

1) How do I place an order for Self Collect?

Just follow these simple steps:

Click on Order Now ----> Select Self Collect ---->Choose your preferred GFC Store ---->Select items from the Self Collect Menu ---->Checkout, pay and collect from your selected GFC store!

2) When is the operating hours for Self Collect?

You can place your order online daily between 7am and 4am. Collection time in-store is available from 11.00am to 10.30pm from Mondays to Sundays including public holidays.

3) What are the payment options available for Self Collect orders?

We accept both debit/credit cards (Visa & Master) issued by banks in Malaysia. Payment is to be made upon order confirmation.

4) How will I know if GFC has received my online order?

An order confirmation email will be sent to your registered email address once your order has been submitted.

5) Where do I collect my orders for Self Collect?

You can collect your order at the GFC store you've selected when you placed your order. There will be a designated area with 'Self Collect' signage in the store where you can pick up your order.Just show your order confirmation details or email to our staff in the store.


Account Management

1) How do I register an account with GFC Delivery?

You will be prompted to 'Log In' or 'Sign Up' when you are going through the ordering process. Select 'Sign Up' to register with us. You can opt for a quick sign up by selecting by entering your email address and details.

Our sign up process is easy and fast, register with us to enjoy the features to ease your orders in the future!

2) How do I reset my password if I have lost or forgotten it?

When you're prompted to log in, select 'Forgot password'. An email containing a link to reset password will be sent to your registered email address.

3) Where can I change my account info or addresses?

First, log into your account. Tap on 'your name' and select 'Profile'. You can edit your personal details in your profile.
